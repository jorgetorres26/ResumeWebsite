I am currently a Junior at the University of Washington’s Information School, specializing in Human Computer Interaction. The Informatics program at the Information School offers me a plethora of skills such as: programming, designing, project management, networking, database management, and web development; similar to a Computer Science program but with a Human-Centered approach. Through my coursework I have experience with both the programming, designing, and research aspects in developing websites and mobile applications. Apart from my technical experience through coursework, I also have experience with working with teams and leadership. In my previous college, I worked as the Program Coordinator for El Centro Latino, a Student Program in Bellevue College (Bellevue, WA) which gave me the opportunity to direct this student program offering services to the college’s Latino students. There I managed the program’s finances, as well as managing projects such as: fundraisers, cultural events and educational trips while helping students in developing their academic and professional skills.
